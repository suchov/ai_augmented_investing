## Fast growers search

### Fins small businesses with Strong Moats Growing at 15%+ Per Year
(moat is difficult to identify)

#### 1 Scan the entire market (US, Ca, UK, EU)

* companies bellow 5 bln
* Revenue CAGR > 10%
* FCF CAGR > 10%
* ROIC > 15%
* Reasonable debt levels

#### 2 AI searches for Moat Signals

* Read 10-K fillings
* Earning call transcripts
* Investor presentations
* Assigne score to each company(switching costs, network effects, brand strength, regulatory
moat, data moat, cost advantage)

#### 3 AI Looks for Proof of the Moats

* stable growth margins(10+ years)
* stable ROIC (15-20% for 10+ years)
* strong consumer retention
* pricing power(raise prices without loosing customers)
* market share stability(competitors are unable to take meaningful share)

#### 5 Build a Compoite Ranking

Excellent buisness questions:
1 Does the business have an identifiable consumer monopoly?
2 Are the earnings of the company strong and showing an upward trend?
3 Is the company conservatively financed?
4 Does the business consistently earn a high rate of return on shareholder's equity?
5 Does the business get to return its earnings?
6 How much does the business have to spend on maintaining current operations?
7 Is the company free of reinvest retained earnings in new business opportunities, expansion of operations, or share repurchases? How good a job does the management do at this?
8 Is the company free to adjust prices to inflation?
9 Will the value added by retained earnings increase the market value of the company?
